“Tsombo” LLC was established in 1996 as an importer of medicines and medical equipment and pharmaceutical product manufacturer in Mongolia. Since then it has been continuously serving in the health sector for 20 years. In 2006, “Tsombo” LLC had completed a manufacturing plant, which produce injection with vial and ampoules and supply public and private hospitals, pharmacies and drug suppliers throughout the country.
